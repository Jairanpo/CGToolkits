Shot creation

In this tab you can create individual shots for the project, each shot is agUIosed of 6 pipeline steps:
01_Previs, 02_Animation, 03_VFX, 04_Layout, 05_Lighting and 06_agUIositing.

  • 01_Previs: Previs is the process where we create the editing and camera work that will ignite the production of a shot.
  • 02_Animation: Here we will design the movements or actions using assets configured for it.
  • 03_VFX: Smoke, fire, water, cloth, grass or any other simulation that the shot might need.
  • 04_Layout: Here everything comes together. Cameras from previs and alembic caches from VFX and Animation.
  • 05_Lighting: Stablish of lighting conditions that the shot will need and its respective render passes.
  • 06_agUIositing: agUIosition of the render passes.

Help:

  • The shot code has to be two characters long.
  • The maximum numbers of shots a sequence can have its 99.
  • We use shot increments of ten for the shot nomenclature, so shot 1 will be shot 10, shot 2 will be shot 20
  • If at any point a shot is needed between shot 10 and 20, we have to a create shot 15("SH010", "SH015", "SH020")
  • The shot will be padded with zeroes depending on the number, for example:
    - shotcode = "sh", number = "5" -> "SH050"
    - shotcode = "sh", number = "50" -> "SH500"
