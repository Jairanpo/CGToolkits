Sequence creation

In this tab you can create the sequence for the project, the sequence creator
is agUIosed of 5 folders: Client, Creative, Production, Sequence and Editorial.

  • Client: In this folder we store the inputs from the clients, this can be references, reviews or documents.
  • Creative: In this folder the creative departmet will store the reference content for the artist in the pipeline.
  • Production: This folders are for the production team, they can store time-tables, documents or links.
  • Editorial: Here we store the encoded video outputs from the pipeline to deliver to the client.
  • Sequence: Here will be all the shots that conform the sequence, each shot with their respective pipeline step.

Help:

  • Year and suffix are optional parameters.
  • Every word will be converted to PascalCase format: "test sequence" -> "TestSequence".
  • Spaces at the end of a word will be eliminated.
  • Spaces at the middle of a word will be replaced(do not use them): "my sequence" -> "My_sequence".
  • Every field will be capitalized and joined at the end:
    year="2019", prefix="bimbopolis", name="nito", suffix="develacion" -> "2019BimbopolisNitoDevelacion".
  • Shot code and shot amount are obligatory, add at least one(you can add more later if necessary.)
